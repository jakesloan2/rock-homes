Privacy & Cookie Policy

How we handle the information you share with us.

This policy explains how Rock Homes ("we", "us") collects and uses personal information when you use this website or contact us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in line with UK data protection law (UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018).

Information we collect

When you complete our enquiry form or contact us, we collect the details you choose to provide — typically your name, email address, phone number and the content of your message. We do not collect special-category personal data through this website.

How we use it

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry, to keep you updated about developments and availability where you have asked us to, and to manage our relationship with you as a prospective purchaser. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Sharing your information

We may share your details with the estate agent appointed to market a development you have enquired about, so they can assist you. Our enquiry form is processed by a third-party form provider on our behalf. Any provider we use is required to keep your data secure and to process it only on our instructions.

How long we keep it

We keep enquiry information only for as long as necessary to deal with your enquiry and any related sales process, after which it is securely deleted.

Cookies

This website uses only the minimal cookies required for the site to function and, where applicable, to understand how the site is used so we can improve it. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies through their settings.

Your rights

You have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, to ask us to correct or delete it, and to object to or restrict how we use it. To exercise any of these rights, or if you have a concern about how we handle your data, please contact us at info@rockhomesni.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.
